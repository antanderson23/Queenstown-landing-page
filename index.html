A note from
the director

This one hits a little different for us at Rosefern.

Hallenstein Street sits close enough to Queenstown's centre that you could walk to the waterfront before your morning coffee goes cold — but far enough that you come home to quiet.

We've designed these eight residences for people who want to be in Queenstown properly, rather than just near it. Close to the lake, close to town, and connected to everything that defines this place: the mountains, the restaurants, the walking tracks, the water, the adrenaline.

The town's topography limits the availability of new land around the most sought-after areas. Investing in a beautifully crafted, time-tested asset class in one of the world's most desirable destinations isn't a leap of faith — it feels like a timely choice your future will thank you for.

— Ant Anderson, Director
